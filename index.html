What is Anime?

Anime is a Japanese term, meaning hand-drawn or computer animation. Just like live action shows and movies, anime has many different genres for everyone's liking. The popular anime genres are Shonen, comedy, and Isekai.


What are some of the anime genres?


-Shonen


Shonen anime has largely been generalized to be series that feature lots of fighting and battles. If you are looking for good shonen anime to watch, you should check out Naruto, One piece, and Bleach.


-Isekai


Isekai anime is an anime where the protagonist gets transfered to another world. This world can range from video games, books and other fantastical places. If you are looking for good Isekai anime to watch, you should check out Sword Art Online, Shield Hero, and Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation.


What are anime's big three?


Something that is unique to the anime community is its big three.The term "Big Three" refers to the animes One Piece, Naruto and Bleach. Their popularity and extensive length meant they frequently featured on the cover of Shonen Jump, with shared covers often featuring characters from all three franchises. The Big Three were the anime that fans were hooked onto the most. All of these animes are all shonens.
