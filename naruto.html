Overview


On the day of Naruto Uzamaki's birth the village of Konoha was attacked by the 9-tailed fox demon. In order to protect the village the 4th hokage (leader of the village) sacrificed his life and sealed the demon in a new born. 13 years later Naruto graduates the ninja academy and becomes a shinobi with goal to be the hokage of the village. Joining him are rival Sasuke Uchiha who attempts to gain power to avenge his clan after they were masicured. And Sakura Haruno who is Naruto's love interest who of course loves his rival Sasuke. But when an evil group returns to the village after the chunnin exams and Sasuke proves to be powerless against them. Sasuke will fall to the villainous Orochimaru to gain power. Naruto must do everything in his power to stop his friend from loosing himself to the darkness even if it means losing himself.
